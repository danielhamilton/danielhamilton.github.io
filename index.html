What I Bring to the Work

Pattern-level strengths that show up across every project and role.

AI-Native Collaboration

I integrate AI tools into creative and technical workflows as thinking partners—strategically, not reactively. I understand where AI genuinely augments work versus where it interrupts it.

Design × Engineering Integration

I move fluently between design and code. Design decisions inform engineering and vice versa. I don’t hand off—I build alongside.

Systems Thinking Across Domains

I see structural connections between design systems, data architecture, narrative, and art history. Better solutions come from understanding the whole system.

Healthcare & Regulated Environments

Deep experience designing under compliance constraints for clinical trials, pharma, and patient-facing systems. Rigor and empathy, not either/or.

Onboarding & Progressive Complexity

I specialize in making overwhelming systems approachable—introducing complexity gradually through clear mental models and well-timed guidance.

Team Leadership & Mentorship

I lead by sharing the spotlight. I build design culture, coach craft, and create optimistic work environments where teams do their best work.
